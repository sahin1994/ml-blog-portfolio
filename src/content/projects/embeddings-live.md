## The problem

Embeddings are the core of modern ML, but they're invisible — thousands of
dimensions you can't see.

## The approach

`embeddings.live` projects them to 2-D in the browser with UMAP and lets you
hover, cluster, and search. Everything runs client-side, so your data never
leaves the page.

## Result

An intuition-builder for what vectors actually encode — shown, not told.
